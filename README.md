## Project Overview
This project demonstrates my ability to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights using Python.
The objective was to analyze sales performance, identify key business metrics, and generate reports that can support better business decisions.

## Business Problem
Businesses often collect sales data but struggle to extract meaningful insights from it.
The goal of this project was to:
* Understand overall sales performance
* Calculate key business KPIs
* Identify trends and patterns
* Create summary reports for decision-making
* Export findings into a professional Excel report

  
## Dataset
The [dataset](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ncSbKWQvZuRHVna1ICpxYq6geYqaQohX/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=104795647098572756661&rtpof=true&sd=true) contained sales transaction records including:
* Customer information
* Products
* Sales revenue
* Transaction dates


## Tools Used
* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Excel

## Analysis Performed
### Data Loading & Exploration
* Imported sales dataset
* Examined structure and data types
* Validated dataset integrity

### Summary Statistics
Generated:
* Total Revenue
* Average Revenue
* Minimum Sale
* Maximum Sale
* Revenue Distribution

### KPI Calculations
Calculated:
* Total Orders
* Total Revenue
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Top Customers
* Top Products

### Pivot Table Analysis
Created pivot tables for:
* Revenue by Product
* Revenue by Customer
* Monthly Sales Performance
* Category Performance

## Business Reporting
Generated a business report highlighting:
* Revenue trends
* Best-performing products
* Top customers

## Excel Export
Exported cleaned analysis and reports into Excel for business use.
